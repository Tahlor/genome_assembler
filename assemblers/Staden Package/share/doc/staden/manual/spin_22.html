Printing the sites

From the Result manager ( see section Result manager), menu a pop-up menu for restriction sites results can be used to write the results in two forms to the the Output window - from here the results can be saved to a file. The two choices of format are "Output enzyme by enzyme" and "Output ordered on position", brief examples of which are shown below. The output also appears in an "Information" window. Note that these listings are also available from gap4.

The restriction enzyme results output ordered "enzyme by enzyme". The enzymes sites are numbered and named and the actual cut site from the sequence is written, followed by the position of the cut, the fragment size, and finally a sorted list of fragment sizes. A list of zero cutters is written underneath.
